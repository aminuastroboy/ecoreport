EcoReport 🌱

Community Environmental Monitoring Platform

Problem

Many communities face illegal dumping, plastic pollution, and blocked drainage but citizens lack a simple way to report environmental problems.

Solution

EcoReport allows citizens to report waste problems, upload photo evidence, mark severity levels, and help identify environmental hotspots.

Impact

Encourages community participation, supports authorities with environmental data, and promotes cleaner communities.
